Surf Corruption is complete!


17th Feb 2020

After a long development process, the wait is finally over, Surf_Corruption has been released.
Development of Corruption started in the summer of 2018, and has eagerly been awaited as one of, if not THE, hardest surf maps.
Oliver, the map maker, was a holder of more than 15 World Records at the time and wanted to push the surf community to a new level.
We caught up with Oliver for his thoughts now the map has been released.



First question, is the map tier 7?

Oliver thinks the map should be considered tier 7 but also that other maps like Goliath and Devil should also be considered tier 7.
We all expect, however, that the tier system isn't going to change just yet and the map will be added as a tier 6.



Why did the map take so long to make?

''I was lazy and bad at hammer so it took a long time, also there were many periods where I completely stopped when I lost motivation"



Why did you make Corruption, What was the motivation?

"After I got the surf_not_so_zen record, I wanted to make another very long and very difficult map, which would play into my skill set, like Not-So-Zen but longer and harder!
I wanted it to take as long to complete as it took to get a decent world record.
I also wanted to make a map that would be considered the hardest map for a good couple years, at least.
It was self serving really, I just wanted a good map that was something fun to surf."



What are the best features of the map?


"Well, I would say 'the surf' even if any inexperienced mapper can make 'good surf' I was trying to take advantage of being able to test the map as often as possible to improve the surf.
I also added 2 crouched head-surfs, but they didn't have a large impact on the map, even if I would like them to have.
They are kinda a gimmicky thing to get used to.
I wanted a head-surf that takes the crouch-surf penalty into account so the surfer would have to be more careful.
I've had people say the ramps are badly placed but I thought the main criticism would be that the map: 'lacks detail, 'has bad lighting' or 'looks boring', because during development it was difficult to change.
I know there is a combination of 'lack of overall detail' and no dynamic lighting but that would take a lot of extra work and testing."



How many hours did it take to make?


"Hours total? I have no idea... A long time for sure.
It's too scattered throughout the 2 years, much longer than it would take any half experienced mapper though."



Why should people surf Corruption?


"Well, its pretty well recognised as one of the hardest maps, and there is always going to be prestige for whoever completes it.
Also, I think the surf is really fun, smooth, semi-awkward, difficult and long.
Another thing is, that a lot of difficult maps don't have curved ramps so Corruption is quite unique.
Most long, difficult, maps don't have curved ramps due to ramp-bugs which kinda sucks for a longer map."



What is the best section?


"Ermm... The tree room. I like how much people struggled with it, it's really fun, and its great that people found it so difficult!"



Oh, well, what is the hardest part of the map?


"If you are using saveloc, probably the tree room; and it might be in run also.
The ending is pretty chokeable but not as bad as the tree room, even though it is likely to feel more difficult due to it being at the end."



How long do you think it will take before someone completes the map?


"Depends... Really difficult to say, people have had access for a long time to earlier versions.
Corruption was made to be the most difficult map, mid to late 2018.
It's 2 years after that and people have gotten a lot better at the game in general as well.
Anyone can put a decent amount of time into it and get it in like 2 weeks, If not earlier, I have no idea how good people have gotten."



It was meant for 2018 difficulty but, didn't you make it harder as you went along?


"Yes, I made it harder, but not to where its up to the standards of today.
I was trying to reach some level above the 2018 standard and by making it harder progressively.
I haven't made it to that level compared to the current skill level."



Name 3 surfers, who you think are the most likely to complete first.


"Rulldar... Levi... Caff. I think Caff has proven himself on longer maps like Looksmodern and I think he has the ability.
It's more of a guess, maybe someone like Saxton or Simex would take spot 3"



Why Snorlax?


"I had this running meme on my stream, because I had this Snorlax pillow in the background as a mascot so I thought it would be fun to put him in the map, especially as I streamed some of the mapping process. It made sense... It had to be done!"



Are you going to complete the map yourself?


"Probably not, unless I come back at some point, but I did complete an earlier version of the map which was a tad easier."
