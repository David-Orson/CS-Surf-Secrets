Advanced: Superior Strafing


In this tutorial, we are going to cover an advanced strafing technique called 'Bounce'.
Bounce is a concept that is only seemingly perfectible by a robot and many people would improve their surf by working on the level of Bounce in their strafes.
Many of the fastest 'unit' surfers and B-Hoppers demonstrate a clear understanding of the concept and have implemented it into their muscle memory.

We will cover a basic explanation of the 'Bounce' concept, and you can try the exercises to implement more Bounce into your strafes!..
